Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What industries do you specialise in?
Financial services, healthcare, technology, energy, consumer goods and industrial sectors — our cross-industry view surfaces solutions others miss.
How long does a typical engagement last?
Diagnostic projects run 4–8 weeks; large-scale transformation programmes span 12–24 months, tailored to your capacity.
Do you work with mid-market companies?
Yes — from $50M+ revenue mid-market firms to Fortune 100 enterprises, delivering institutional-quality insight at every scale.
How are fees structured?
Fixed-project, retainer, or performance-based models. Full transparency — detailed scope and cost proposal before any work begins.
How do you protect confidential information?
Mutual NDAs from day one, strict information barriers between client teams, and ISO 27001-aligned data security protocols.
Can we engage for a single scoped project?
Absolutely. Many long-term partnerships began as a single focused assessment or market-entry study — no ongoing commitment required.
